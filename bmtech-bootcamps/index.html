Your Journey

What are the key points of the bootcamp experience?

  • Phase 1

    Training - Ground Zero

    In this foundational phase, students will undergo comprehensive training to establish a solid understanding of programming basics. From language fundamentals to algorithmic thinking, this phase serves as the launchpad for their coding journey.

  • Phase 2

    Coding Like An Engineer

    Building upon the fundamentals, this phase delves into advanced coding techniques and engineering principles. Students will learn to think like an engineer, mastering the art of designing efficient and scalable solutions to real-world problems.

  • Phase 3

    Create Your Own Project

    Putting theory into practice, this phase empowers students to conceive and execute their own coding projects. From ideation to implementation, students will gain hands-on experience, honing their skills while bringing their creative ideas to life.

  • Phase 4

    Landing Your Dream Tech Job

    In the final phase, students focus on career readiness. They'll learn about industry best practices, participate in mock interviews, and receive guidance on creating an impressive portfolio. The goal is to equip them with the skills and confidence needed to secure their dream tech job upon completion of the bootcamp.
